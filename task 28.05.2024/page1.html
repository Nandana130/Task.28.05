Coffee brewing methods

    1.Espresso


Ingredients
  • boiling water
  • coffee beans

      • Espresso is a coffee-brewing method in which a small amount of nearly boiling water
      is forced under pressure through finely ground coffee beans. Originating from Italy,
      the French also made significant contributions via the invention of coffee makers,
      predecessors of today's espresso machines.

      2.French press


    Ingredients
  • boiling water
  • coffee beans

      • A French press, also known as a cafetière, cafetière à piston, caffettiera a stantuffo,
      press pot, coffee press, or coffee plunger, is a coffee brewing device, although it can also be
      used for other tasks. The earliest known device was patented in 1852 in France by Jacques-Victor
      Delforge and Henri-Otto Mayer
